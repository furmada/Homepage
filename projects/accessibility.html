Accessibility

An experimental device for the blind.

What is this project?

This is an experimental device made from an Arduino, distance sensor, and a speaker. It is mounted on a white cane, and scans the distance to obstacles and generates a tone according to distance. It is intended to be cheap, portable, and extendable.
