What is Open Flashcards?

Open Flashcards is a Windows 8 app for making, organizing, and studying flash cards. Featuring a clear and simple cards layout, intuitive knowledge-based study mode, and import/export features. With Open Flashcards, you can:

  • Make custom flashcards
  • Organize them into categories
  • Import and export cards
  • Study cards based on your knowledge

Study Mode will ask you if you know a card, and put cards you don't know back into the study pile.
