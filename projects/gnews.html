What is GNews?

GNews, or News Reader for Google News, is a news reader app built with modern design in mind. Available as a web page, Firefox app, or Windows 8.1 app, GNews offers a seamless browsing and reading experience. With GNews, you can:

  • Browse aricles across categories
  • Read articles inside the app or in your web browser
  • *Share articles
  • *Add custom categories

* indicates a platform-dependent feature.
